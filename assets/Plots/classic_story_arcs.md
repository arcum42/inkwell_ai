# Classic Story Arcs

## The Hero's Journey (Monomyth)

1. **Ordinary World** - Hero's normal life
2. **Call to Adventure** - Event that starts the story
3. **Refusal of the Call** - Fear, doubt, hesitation
4. **Meeting the Mentor** - Guide/teacher appears
5. **Crossing the Threshold** - Commitment to the quest
6. **Tests, Allies, Enemies** - Learning the rules
7. **Approach to the Inmost Cave** - Preparation for major challenge
8. **Ordeal** - Central crisis/greatest fear
9. **Reward** - Achievement/survival
10. **The Road Back** - Journey home with new knowledge
11. **Resurrection** - Final test using everything learned
12. **Return with the Elixir** - Transform the ordinary world

## Three-Act Structure

### Act 1: Setup
- Introduce world, characters, and central conflict
- Build to inciting incident that launches the story

### Act 2: Confrontation  
- Hero faces obstacles and challenges
- Relationships develop, stakes rise
- Midpoint: Major revelation or setback

### Act 3: Resolution
- Climax: Confrontation of central conflict
- Denouement: Consequences and new normal
