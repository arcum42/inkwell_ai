# Types of Conflict

## Man vs. Self
Internal struggle with emotions, doubts, fears, or moral dilemmas.

**Examples:**
- Character overcoming fear to achieve goal
- Moral choice between two principles
- Mental health struggles

## Man vs. Man
Conflict between two or more people with opposing goals.

**Examples:**
- Rival professionals competing
- Family disputes
- Enemies in warfare

## Man vs. Society
Character or group at odds with cultural norms or systems.

**Examples:**
- Dystopian rebellion
- Social injustice
- Outcasts from community

## Man vs. Nature
Struggle against environmental forces or creatures.

**Examples:**
- Survival in harsh environment
- Natural disaster
- Animal attack

## Man vs. Fate/Unknown
Conflict with forces beyond human control or understanding.

**Examples:**
- Supernatural mystery
- Illness or curse
- Cosmic horror

## Combining Conflicts
The strongest stories often layer multiple types of conflict together.
