# Setting Details Checklist

## Geography
- [ ] Location (continent, country, city, neighborhood)
- [ ] Climate and seasons
- [ ] Landscape features (mountains, forests, rivers, deserts)
- [ ] Natural resources available
- [ ] Isolated or connected to other places?

## Architecture & Infrastructure
- [ ] Building styles
- [ ] Technology level
- [ ] Transportation methods
- [ ] Communication systems
- [ ] Public spaces and landmarks

## Government & Authority
- [ ] Type of government
- [ ] Who holds power?
- [ ] Laws and enforcement
- [ ] Justice system
- [ ] Economic system

## Culture & Society
- [ ] Languages spoken
- [ ] Holidays and celebrations
- [ ] Religious beliefs
- [ ] Social classes or hierarchy
- [ ] Gender roles
- [ ] Coming of age rituals

## Daily Life
- [ ] Food and dining customs
- [ ] Clothing and fashion
- [ ] Entertainment
- [ ] Courtship/marriage customs
- [ ] Family structures

## History
- [ ] Major historical events
- [ ] Ancient civilizations
- [ ] Recent conflicts
- [ ] Cultural origins
- [ ] How it's changed over time

## Unique Elements
- [ ] What makes this setting distinctive?
- [ ] What would surprise an outsider?
- [ ] What are locals proud of?
- [ ] What do they struggle with?

## Sensory Details
- [ ] Sights - What does it look like?
- [ ] Sounds - What do you hear?
- [ ] Smells - Distinctive scents?
- [ ] Textures - How things feel?
- [ ] Tastes - Local flavors?
